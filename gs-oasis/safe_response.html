Educational Information

Scams often involve tactics to pressure you into revealing personal or financial information. They may ask you for immediate action, contain threats or warnings, present an unexpected reward, contain links to suspicious websites, or have grammar and spelling errors. Always stay vigilant and question any unexpected communications. Never share personal or financial information unless you can verify the source and it's essential for a legitimate purpose.

Red Flags to Watch For

  • Unexpected requests for money
  • Threats or excessive urgency
  • Poor spelling and grammar
  • Requests for personal information
  • Suspicious or unrecognized links
