How GS Oasis Protects You

Image Scanning Protection

Our advanced AI can detect various types of fraudulent images:

  • Fake product listings with stolen or manipulated images
  • Deepfakes used in sophisticated scams
  • QR codes that lead to malicious websites
  • Hidden text or metadata containing harmful content

Link Verification System

Our link scanner protects you from:

  • Phishing websites that steal your personal information
  • Fake banking or e-commerce sites
  • Shortened URLs hiding malicious destinations
  • Sites containing browser exploits or drive-by downloads

File Analysis Engine

Our file scanner identifies threats including:

  • Ransomware that could lock your files and demand payment
  • Trojans disguised as legitimate software
  • Malicious macros in document files
  • Zero-day threats not yet recognized by traditional antivirus
