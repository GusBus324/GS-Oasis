Frequently Asked Questions

How does GS Oasis protect me online?

GS Oasis provides tools to scan and verify images, links, and files for potential scams and security threats. Our AI-powered system analyzes content for common scam indicators and provides information to help you make safer decisions online.

Is my data safe with GS Oasis?

We take data privacy seriously. All scans are processed securely, and we don't store your scanned content permanently. Your personal information is protected according to our Privacy Policy, and we never share it with third parties.

How accurate is the scam detection?

Our scam detection system has a 98% accuracy rate. However, new scam techniques emerge constantly, so we always encourage users to exercise caution even when our system doesn't detect a threat.

Is there a limit to how many scans I can perform?

Standard accounts can perform up to 50 scans per day. If you need more, please contact us about our premium options.
